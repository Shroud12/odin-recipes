Chicken Mo:Mo Recipe

Description

Momo is a Nepalese style dumpling which are eaten as snacks. They are made with a tasty filling primarily consisting of ground chicken.

Ingredients

  • Pre made dumpling wrapper
  • Ground chicken meat - 500 grams
  • Red Onion - 1
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 teaspoons
  • Tumeric - 1 teaspoon
  • Chilly Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Momo masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - User preference
  • Mustard oil - 2 teaspoons

Steps to follow :

    To prepare the filling follow the following steps:

  1. Take a decent size mixing bowl.
  2. Add 500 grams of ground chicken.
  3. Finely chop the red onion and add to the bowl.
  4. Now add the ginger garlic paste to the bowl.
  5. Add tumeric, chilly powder, momo masala, salt and mustard oil as listed above in the ingredients section.
  6. Add one teaspoon of water, this will keep our meat tender while cooking.
  7. Now mix all the ingredients together.
P.S : You can find momo masala in any Asian grocery store.
