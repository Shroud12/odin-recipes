Nepali Chicken Curry

Description :

Let us take a look at how a simple and delicious chicken curry is made in Nepal. This dish is generally prepared in weekends and enjoyed with the whole family.

Ingredients :

  • Chicken - 500 grams.
  • Red Onion - 2.
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tablepoons.
  • Tumeric - 1 teaspoon.
  • Green Chilly - 2.
  • Medium sized tomato - 4-5
  • Mustard oil - 4 tablepoons.
  • Chicken Masala - 1 and half tablespoons.
  • Salt - To taste.

Steps to follow :

  1. Firstly roughly dice your onions, chillies and tomatoes and set them aside
  2. Open your stove on medium-low heat and place a pan.
  3. Pour 2 tablespoons of mustard oil and let it heat up a little.
  4. After heating up the oil throw in the chicken.
  5. Cook the chicken thoroughly until it has a little golden crust on the outside. Around 15-16 minutes.
  6. Remove the chicken in a bowl and set it aside for now.
  7. In the same pan add 1 tablespoon of mustard oil.
  8. Now pour in your roughly choped onions first.
  9. Add a pinch of salt to help wilt the onions faster.
  10. Add the chillies to the onions and also add tumeric to give it some color.
  11. Cook the onions till the oil spererates from it and remember to keep on stirring them to prevent them from burning.
  12. Now add in the tomatoes and again a small pinch of salt to help it cook faster. Cook them weel otherwise your curry will taste like raw tomatoes
  13. Cook the tomatoes till it completely wilts and chunks start disappearaing.
  14. Now toss in the chicken that you set aside to the pan. Since we already cooked the chicken before we will only cook it for 6-7 more minutes.
  15. Finally add the chicken masala and the chilly powder to the curry and throughly mix them. Cook it for additional 2-3 minutes.
  16. The chicken curry is now ready. You can eat it with a serving of rice or with flat-breads
