Nepali Lassi

Description :

Lassi is a yogurt based drink that is simple to make. It helps you cool your body in a hot summer day.

Ingredients :

  • Plain yogurt - 2 cups
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Cardamom - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Ice - 2 cups

Steps to follow :

  1. Take a blender, put all the ingredients and blend them till a smooth fixture is obtained.
  2. Serve it immediately on a glass.
